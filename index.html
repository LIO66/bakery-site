ABOUT US

We’ve baked every item from the core of our heart to serve you

I am a master baker originating from Dehradun, India. I began baking at the age of 17 as a hobby that turned into passion. Over the next 3 years, I worked and learned the skills and recipes of the that delight every taste buds. At the age of 20, I became a owner of Miss Baker Queen and have been baking amazing recipes to qualify for such a title. On January 1st, 2020, I will celebrate 3 years of baking experience. Over these years, I had the pleasure of taking my skills to people of my hometown. I baked for my friends, family and lovely people of my hometown gaining valuable experience learning new techniques, modern recipes and bakery management skills.I became quite knowledgeable about the Baking culture, food and types of bakery products. After two year of baking practices for friends & family, I began the journey of running my own company, Miss Baker Queen. My elder brother Navraj and parents enjoy helping with the business and I look forward to passing on my baking knowledge to the people and let them get indulge in superb baking recipes from Miss Baker Queen.
